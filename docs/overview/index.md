## Why Dataspace Ecosystem?

The Dataspace Ecosystem addresses the growing need for organizations to share data in a controlled, policy-governed, and auditable manner, without relying on a centralized intermediary.

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Data Sovereignty** | Data owners retain full control over who accesses their data and under what conditions |
| **Decentralization** | No single entity controls the ecosystem. Peer-to-peer exchange with federated governance |
| **Interoperability** | Built on open standards (IDS, ODRL, W3C DID/VC) for seamless cross-organization integration |
| **Trust** | Verifiable credentials, policy enforcement, and cryptographic verification ensure trusted exchanges |
